##Subject
ID of the person involved in the experiment (1-30)

##Activity
Type of activity (one of six choices) e.g. Walking

## Measurements
Most Aggregated (grouped) measurements are represented as [Measurement]-[mean or std dev]-[x, y or z]
Some measurements are not three dimensional and so will only be represented as [Measurement]-[mean or std. dev]
